About Us

Welcome to Carolina Chords, where the melody of your guitar journey begins with a single strum! Nestled in the heart of a music-loving and welcoming community known as UNC (University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill), we stand as a beacon for aspiring guitarists of all ages and skill levels, offering a sanctuary where passion meets practice, or just a place for guitarists and those who are interested to collaborate and hang out.

Our team comprises seasoned instructors, each a virtuoso in their own right, fueled by an unwavering love for music and a dedication to nurturing talent. With personalized attention and comprehensive guidance, we craft lessons tailored to your unique strengths and aspirations, ensuring that every chord struck resonates with purpose and progress. Our instructors are people just like you, thus eliminating the fear factor in learning guitar lessons.

At Carolina Chords, we firmly believe that within each individual lies a distinct musical voice yearning to be heard. Join our ever-growing family of musicians, where camaraderie thrives, creativity flourishes, and every note played contributes to the symphony of collective learning. Let us be your guides as you explore the vast expanse of musical expression, discover new rhythms, hone your skills, and revel in the joyous harmony of mastering the guitar in a vibrant and supportive environment. Together, let's embark on a journey where every strum brings us closer to the melody of our souls.

Most of our lessons start at $50 a session, but your first session is always free to test out the waters and see if you like Carolina Chords.

Fill out our sign up form and inform us if you are a prospective instructor or a prospective student. Everyone is welcome to this community.
